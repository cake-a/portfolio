violation, 10/19/25

We have another ceasefire violation.

Why are we allowing the eradication of a nation?

Palestinians, the descendants of the original melting pot, Jerusalem.

Not that it matters, none of this should be happening to any human.


Name a hundred people you know and imagine that they're dead.

Even one or two civilians are enough, the Palestinians are struggling to be fed.

Traumatized children and babies birthed under rubble.

Starving for a bit of milk from a malnourished mothers nipple.


Withholding food and medicine from the young, sick, and disabled; fear of a group of rebel farmers.

By doing so, Israel killed their own hostages, permanent marker.

Destroying historical landmarks older than the state of Israel.

Honestly, just give it up already. Jerusalem is not for sale.


Israel has become worst than Hitler and Pharoah.

Keep trying to wrestle with God, Israel's foe.

What God wouldn't punish bloodthirsty savages.

God of Abraham, make sure they pay for all the damages.


For destroying hope for peace and justice between all mankind.

I'm ashamed to be of the same species, peace in death I shall find.
